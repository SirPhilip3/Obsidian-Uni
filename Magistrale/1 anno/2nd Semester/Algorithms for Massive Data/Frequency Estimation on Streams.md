>[!important] Problem Definition
>Given a stream :
>$$
>x_{1},\dots,x_{m}
>$$
>
>Where $x_{i} \in [n]$, build a *data sketch* that allows us to estimate for any $y \in [n]$ :
>$$
>f_{y} = |{i : x_{i} = y}|
>$$
>
>Or the **frequency** ( number of occurrences ) of $y$ in the stream
